Our Process

Responsible 100 helps all businesses to better understand the sustainability and responsibility issues they face. We help assess current performance, determine which practices to change and improve, and how.  

Responsible 100 helps any business to:  

>> Clearly identify and better understand the various sustainability and responsibility issues which affect it, and which it affects 

>> Identify the issues which matter most, and those where they have the greatest opportunity to positively impact people and planet  

>> Map out the possible pathways for achieving better social and environmental practices and outcomes 

>> Measure and monitor progress using open-sourced performance benchmarks 

>> Report internally and externally 

Responsible 100 also supports businesses through every step and in making changes and improvements to their practices.

The R100 Badge

Businesses that actively and continually seek to balance their pursuit of profit and business excellence, with the needs of people and planet, need due recognition and support.

By providing real choice for consumers, employees and investors who want to help create and be part of a better kind of business, we create economic reward for pursuing real responsibility. As well as creating the critical economic incentives for other businesses to follow suit.

By connecting the businesses that are best for the world with all those people who wish to see them succeed, we can make real responsibility an increasingly powerful driver of business success.

Responsible 100 is exploring various ways in which participating businesses can publicise their participation. A badge or kitemark, alongside the provision of statements of commitment to critical issues, in the form of assets for publication are a distinct possibility.
