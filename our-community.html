OUR COMMUNITY
"Responsible 100 has perfected the art of bringing companies and NGOs together to explore and share challenges, learning and practical know how, in a safe space. It's for people who are passionate about making a difference. Not for those who want to talk through the theory of how to make a difference, but those keen to learn about what works in today's context to ready their organisations for the future. "
Head of Corporate Responsibility & Employee Engagement, Virgin Trains

Our community blends the conscience of civil society with the innovation and problem-solving skills of business.

Responsible 100 maps out the possible pathways to achieve better social and environmental practices and outcomes. Through a safe, supportive and highly efficient process, we take any business through simple steps of discovery and assessment against open-sourced performance benchmarks, which are themselves the products of collaborative endeavour.

We create safe, collaborative and supportive environments in which to share knowledge and improve together.

We’ve made thousands of connections, built relationships and grown a network. We're a community of people and organisations which share a passion for unlocking the vast potential of business to help build and be part of a better world.

Exploration sparks our imaginations. It leads to breakthrough ideas and inspires innovation. Appreciating what really matters most and what makes the most difference is crucial. But so too is learning from instances when, despite best efforts, little or no difference has been made, or when a business has simply failed to find alternatives to POOR practices.

Knowledge sharing requires the socialising of challenges, and identifying and prioritising ideas with the greatest potential to create real change. Working together, identifying opportunities and co-creating and trialling solutions has proved to be highly productive and stimulating work. And it has provided the glue to bind a vibrant and growing community together.

LARGE BUSINESSES

Responsible 100 offers a unique process for pathfinding and strategy optimisation. We support corporations and large organisations, irrespective of their sector or starting point, through valuable processes of engagement, discovery, assessment, disclosure and continuous improvement.

Entry points differ business to business. Larger organisations are often willing and able to include stakeholders, and employees in particular, through the various steps of the Responsible 100 process, to the benefit of all.

“We try to be transparent on our tax approach and tax in general has become a topic which is under regular media and civic society attention. Therefore it’s a very valuable exercise to be ‘critically friended’. It lets us learn where we are getting it right, where more would be appreciated and gives us a variety of constructive feedback directly from a diverse group of informed yet critical stakeholders."
Chief Tax Officer, FTSE100 corporation
SMEs & STARTUPS

We proudly work with a wide range of small and medium-sized enterprises. We'll help your SME to be more responsible, and for your increased responsibility to drive commercial success and financial results. Starting this process in the earlier stages of business growth will provide a solid foundation to make responsible practices sit at the heart of your operations.

Understanding and addressing a constantly evolving set of pressing social, environmental, ethical and governance issues is a complex and often overwhelming undertaking for any business. We help you to better understand the responsibility challenges you face, how well you perform, and what you can do to improve.

"Responsible 100 is a brilliant initiative. The opportunity for  peer learning around real responsibility issues is so valuable. The deep dives ensure a really good level of detail and tangible and realistic actions afterwards."
Co-founder, Pukka Herbs
SUPPORTER ORGANISATIONS

More than ever, it is essential that the global challenges the world faces are championed by passionate NGOs and campaigners . Responsible 100 enables campaign groups to raise their issues right up to the top of boardroom agendas in businesses right around the world.

We have a proud record of bringing those holding corporate power to account together with the very businesses they scrutinise. Supporter organisations are integral to our approach and model, and to identifying and influencing positive change in business.
