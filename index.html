SUPPORTING & REWARDING
ALL BUSINESSES
SERVING PEOPLE & PLANET
What if ANY business was able to maximise its positive impacts on people and planet, and minimise its negative impacts, while delivering the goods and services we all need?

What if any one of us, as a consumer, an employee or an investor, could identify those businesses and reward them with our purchases, labour and investment?

We believe the result would be profound. And that maximising ‘net positive’ impacts on people and planet would become the profit-maximising choice for almost all businesses. Further, there’d be powerful economic incentives for ALL businesses to do more good, and less bad, to the fullest possible extent.

Responsible 100’s purpose is to support and enable any business seeking to make that choice. And to empower all those who wish to identify, support and reward those businesses, and create and be part of an economy that safeguards the environment and serves all of society.

In August 2020 the pioneering beer company BrewDog announced it had become carbon negative, and that it was removing twice as much carbon from the air than it emits. Enabling the claim: “by drinking BrewDog beer, you're having a positive impact on the planet”. Great news for beer drinkers, and great news for the planet!

So which other businesses are producing the goods and services we all need and want - while also seeking to fix the huge problems we face?

Certainly, we face a climate crisis, but also crises of poverty, of modern slavery in supply chains, of biodiversity loss, of pollution, of rampant inequality, of worsening mental health and wellbeing, and of fracturing democracy and systemic racism too.

These are truly wicked problems, and they're faced the world over. They demand that we all play our part. And we CAN....!

Responsible 100 enables all businesses to start being part of the solution - and stop adding further to existing problems - as fast and as far across their organisations as they possibly can.

We work with any company, no matter its size, sector or starting point. Our process allows discovery and education on all the responsibility issues that matter, working out which issues to prioritise now. We determine current performance, and then provide the practical know-how and support to improve - as quickly and comprehensively as the limitations of being a viable, profitable business allow.

We help businesses fix what matters most. None can be EXCELLENT on every issue. But any business can cease POOR practices and discover and choose the pathway to OKAY, GOOD and beyond.

Responsible 100 enables any business to practice responsibility as a skill: to increase its knowledge, competence and confidence, to deploy its resources ever more efficiently and effectively, and to incrementally yet consistently improve its responsibility performance - on more and more issues. Crucially, we translate improved responsibility performance into improved profitability.

The ultimate test will be delivering their services profitability and at scale, and I'll bet on them achieving success and having extraordinary impact on the world."
