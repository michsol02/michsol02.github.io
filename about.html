ABOUT US

Responsible 100's mission is to make real responsibility the profit-maximising choice for any business.

We help any business to be more responsible. For its own benefit, and for the benefit of everyone else. We enable all businesses to compete in a race to the top: not only on price and quality, but also on how positively they can impact people and planet. Our goal is to make any business more responsible and more profitable as a result.

TAKE RESPONSIBILITY
RESCUE PROSPERITY

We have a choice. Two paths lay ahead of us. One is perilous, the other full of promise. One leads us to climate change, and eventual ecological and societal collapse. The other requires systemic change, and leads us to a healthy, prosperous future. 

It is a simple choice: change or be changed. The first path – be changed – is the path we are on today. The ideas and assumptions that have given us so much prosperity to date are failing us.

If we continue with them, if we don’t take responsibility for their failure, our prosperity will not last. If we want a prosperous future for ourselves, our families and our descendents, then we need to choose to change, now.

Choices like this are rare. The chance to shift and accelerate the direction of human progress, to rescue us from the jaws of failure, to define a new era of prosperity for all. That is a once in a lifetime opportunity.

We need to take responsibility for this choice. Choosing to change means choosing to act responsibly.

To choose the path of promise, of profit, of prosperity, we must be responsible. We know we must act.

We know we must uncover exactly what changes to make and how to make them to create and be part of the future we know in our hearts is possible.

We know we can do it.

Responsible 100 helps any business - no matter its size, sector or starting point - to better understand, control and direct its responsibility performance and impacts.  

We support businesses in a process of continuous improvement. We enable them to maximise their net positive impacts on people and planet, while delivering the goods and service the world wants.

Responsible 100 helps businesses to realise their huge untapped potential to make the world a better place, and to steer mankind towards a fair, livable and sustainable future. 

We don’t ask businesses to reach a certain grade or arbitrary milestones, to receive a certificate, to then sit back and bathe in the glory. We offer practical tools and support to help any business to strike its optimal balance between is pursuit of commercial goals and responsibility to society and the environment. For us, responsibility is a skill and an ongoing commitment. It is a driver of operational efficiency and a risk mitigator. It is a powerful tool for innovation, stakeholder engagement, networking and for nurturing and developing organisational culture.

Responsible 100 is run - and was conceived and developed - by Profit Through Ethics Ltd, a for-good, for-profit business based in London, UK.
